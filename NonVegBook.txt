Chicken Curry
INGREDIENTS : chicken pieces,yogurt,ginger-garlic paste,red chili powder,salt,oil,onions,tomatoes,curry powder,masala powder,coriander powder,water.
DIRECTIONS : Marinate chicken in a mix of yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, turmeric, red chili powder, and salt for at least 1 hour.Add oil pureed tomatoes and optional tomato paste; cook until the oil separates.Stir in curry powder, cumin powder, coriander powder, and garam masala; sauté for a couple of minutes.Add marinated chicken, cook until browned.Pour in water, add salt, simmer until chicken is cooked and curry thickens.
