Veg Soup
INGREDIENTS : Mixed vegetables,olive oil,water,garlic,Salt and pepper
DIRECTIONS : Saute a mix of onions,carrots in olive oil until softened.Add chopped garlic and cook briefly.Pour in vegetable broth, add diced potatoes, tomatoes, and a mix of your favorite veggies.Season with salt, pepper, and herbs; simmer until vegetables are tender.Optionally, blend part of the soup for a thicker texture.
